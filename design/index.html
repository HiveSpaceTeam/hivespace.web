3Principles · follow these when designing

1Use tokens, not vibes

Every color, radius and shadow lives in colors_and_type.css. If you think you need a new color, derive it in OKLCH from --brand-500 — don't invent from scratch.

2Montserrat is the voice

All three surfaces use Montserrat (Inter as a platform fallback). Don't swap for stylistic reasons — it's part of the brand signature.

3Radii are disciplined

rounded-lg (8px) for controls and buttons. rounded-xl (12px) for cards. rounded-full for pills, avatars and icon-buttons. That's it.

4Cards use border, not shadow

border border-gray-200 bg-white is the default card. Reserve the shadow tokens for floating surfaces: modals, popovers, toasts.

5Density matches the audience

Admin & Seller: h-11 inputs, px-5 py-3 buttons, dense tables. Storefront breathes more — larger hero type, generous gutters, richer imagery.

6Status colors are semantic

success/warning/error/info are never decoration. Don't reach for --success-500 to make something feel fresh.
